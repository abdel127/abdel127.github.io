3D Canvas

Concept:

The world is your canvas. You can create billboards/poles by drawing a line that starts at the ground and ends at the sky. You can draw clouds and birds by starting lines at the sky and ending them at the sky. Boards/posts start at billboards and end anywhere. The sky drawings stay in the sky as the user moves, but the poles are closer objects where the user can walk towards them.

Written in Visual Studio C++ with the MinGfx library.
